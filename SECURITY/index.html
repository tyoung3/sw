Reporting a Vulnerability

E-mail streamwork_security@twyoung.com to report a vulnerability.

Please do not expect acknowlegement or e-mail responses of any kind. Watch the github repo for appropriate updates.

There are currently no known security problems. We greatly appreciate your help in keeping things that way.
Let us know in your e-mail if you wish to be publicly acknowledged, or not.
